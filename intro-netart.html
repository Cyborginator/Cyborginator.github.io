Net Art

For this I designed a website using HTML and a little bit of CSS for the style. I figured HTML would be the perfect medium for a quiz since I could link to a different page if the user got a right or wrong answer. This way the quiz can track how many answers they got right and show them the results at the end. In addition to that I also made the CSS background color change to red or green depending on whether the answer was right or not.

I chose memes for the quiz topic since I know a lot about them and making it would be both fun and let me put some of my personality into the responses.
