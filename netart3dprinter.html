Net Art 3D Printer

The concept I am exploring in this project is how a website advertising a modern current day technology, in this case a 3D printer, would have looked like if it were released in the early days of the internet. I made the pages simple and colorful as most web pages did back in the day, and have a page where the video gets more and more distorted to give the effect of going more into the past and to more confuser the viewer.
